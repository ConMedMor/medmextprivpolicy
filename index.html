MaxContact Siren – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15/08/2025

MaxContact Siren is a Chrome extension for Medmark employees that plays an audible alert when an incoming call is detected on the MaxContact dashboard (medmark.maxcontact.com).

Data We Access

  • Microphone: Requested only when you choose to test or configure audio devices. No audio is recorded or transmitted.
  • Audio Device Information: We read available audio input/output devices so you can select one for alerts.
  • Local Storage: We save your selected audio output device (and related settings) locally in Chrome via the chrome.storage API.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal data or call content is sent off your device.
  • No analytics, tracking, advertising, or profiling.
  • No sale or sharing of data with third parties.

Use of Permissions

  • Host permissions for https://medmark.maxcontact.com/* allow the extension to detect the incoming-call modal to trigger alerts.
  • Microphone prompts occur only when you initiate device testing/configuration using standard browser APIs.

Data Retention & Control

  • Settings stored via chrome.storage remain on your device and can be cleared by resetting the extension’s settings or removing the extension.

Children’s Privacy

This extension is intended for Medmark employees and is not directed to children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Material changes will update the “Last updated” date above.
